Wire and Water

Bill Hart

This is an analogue video I made as a second year BFA student - its still a favourite of mine.

You don't have to have sophisticated digital technology to create visual effects. Simple keying and shooting through a fish tank filled with water creates the rippling text.

One of the most interesting trends in the past few years is the move to working with hybrid approaches to media.
