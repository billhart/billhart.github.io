Bill Hart - Art and Technology

Trespass

 

Trespass, 1992, Another analogue video I made as a second year BFA student.   Shot in and around Remarkable Cave, Tasman Peninsula, Tasmania. A bunch of local boys having fun throwing video cameras off cliffs and also considering their identity at a time of emerging awareness of indigenous culture. Tasman peninsula is a special place; site of extraordinary beauty, massacres, brutal penal colonies and honeymooners swept from the rocks by freak waves.
